Biography

I am an assistent professor in the Social Psychology department of Tilburg University. My research interests include the origin and maintenance of false beliefs, assessing physiological arousal using pupillometry, and experimental design in general.

I teach several courses, among them are Social Psychology, Experimental Design, and Attitudes & Advertising. I also provide workshops on R, a statistical programming language.

On this website you will find my publications and talks, as well as the projects that I am involved in. It is also my intent to blog about these projects and provide reuseable code.
